Privacy Policy

Word Duel

Last updated: April 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Word Duel ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application. By using Word Duel, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Account information: email address and username when you sign up
  • Gameplay data: match history, words submitted, scores, and rankings
  • Device information: device type and operating system, used for ad serving and crash reporting
  • Purchase information: subscription status and transaction history for in-app purchases (processed by RevenueCat and the App Store / Google Play — we never see your payment details)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To run real-time multiplayer matches
  • To display the global leaderboard
  • To process in-app purchases and verify Pro subscription status
  • To serve advertisements (non-Pro users only)
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

3. Third-Party Services

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Children's Privacy

Word Duel is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
